## 🔧 How to Test

### Step 1: Open the Website
Navigate to: `index.html` in your browser

### Step 2: Click Language Toggle
Look for the toggle button in the top navigation:
```
[EN | العربية]
```

### Step 3: Switch to Arabic
Click on **"العربية"**

### What You Should See:
✅ **ALL text changes to Arabic** (not English text aligned to the right)
✅ **Layout flips to RTL** (right-to-left reading direction)
✅ **Font changes to Tajawal** (Arabic-optimized font)
✅ **Navigation menu reverses** (Home becomes الرئيسية on the right)
✅ **Buttons flip** (icons move to correct positions)
✅ **Forms align right** (proper Arabic form layout)

### Step 4: Switch Back to English
Click on **"EN"**

✅ Everything returns to English with LTR layout
